# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Loomi is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

We expect all contributors to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Provide helpful feedback
- Avoid harassment or personal attacks
- Focus on improving the project

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or disruptive comments
- Publishing private information

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

Let’s build Loomi with professionalism and respect.
